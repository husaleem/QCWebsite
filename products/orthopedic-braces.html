Orthopedic Braces

Find Relief from Back and Joint Pain — Covered by Medicare!

Back, Knee, and Joint Support

Back, knee, joint, and wrist pain are extremely common and can interfere with daily life. Lower back pain especially can stem from poor posture, injuries, excess weight, or even sleeping in the wrong position.

For chronic or severe cases, Medicare-covered back braces can provide real relief. These devices help stabilize the spine, reduce strain, and support healing from injury or surgery. You may qualify at no cost through your insurance—call us today to learn more.

TRU-RANGE KNEE

Designed with multiple range-of-motion (ROM) settings, TRU-RANGE KNEE supports recovery from:

  • ACL and MCL injuries
  • Cartilage and meniscus tears
  • Patella and tibia/fibula fractures
  • Hyperextension and ligament derangements
  • Osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis
  • Multiple sclerosis and sprains
  • Post-surgery rehab and knee rehabilitation
