Freestyle Libre 2

Discreet and painless, the FreeStyle Libre 14-day sensor monitors glucose continuously—even through clothing. It's water-resistant and provides accurate insulin dosing insights.

With the FreeStyle LibreLink app, you can view readings on your phone. Share data using LibreLinkUp with up to 20 trusted people for real-time support.

Note: While the app and reader offer similar features, they’re not identical. Fingersticks may be required when readings seem inaccurate or symptoms don’t match the data.
